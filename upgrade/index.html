Common questions

Do you charge per transaction?

No. ChowHub charges a flat monthly fee only. You keep 100% of every sale.

What hardware do I need?

ChowHub runs on any device with a browser — a tablet, laptop, or Raspberry Pi. No proprietary terminals required.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrades are instant. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens if I lose internet?

ChowHub runs locally on your hardware. You can take orders and process cash payments with no internet connection.

How do I get my license key?

After subscribing, your license key will be emailed to you. Enter it during installation or in the POS settings.
