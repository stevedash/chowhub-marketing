Where ChowHub wins

Cost, freedom, resilience.

  • Roughly one-fifth the monthly cost at comparable feature level
  • No 2–3 year lock-in — cancel any month
  • No processor lock-in — your Stripe / Square rate is yours
  • Runs on a $75 Raspberry Pi or any laptop you already own
  • Transparent published pricing — no "call sales for a quote"
  • Self-hostable for venues that don't want their data on a vendor's cloud
Where Toast wins

Be honest — credibility matters.

  • Best-in-class first-party handhelds with integrated payment (Toast Go 2)
  • Native DoorDash Drive dispatch — huge for delivery-heavy concepts
  • Toast Payroll, Toast Capital, loyalty and marketing all in one vendor
  • Dedicated account management and 24/7 restaurant-specialist support
  • Cohort analysis, menu engineering, labor forecasting — deep enterprise reporting
  • Massive ecosystem of restaurant-vendor integrations

Best fit for ChowHub

Independent restaurants, cafés, bars, and 2–5 location groups who want a real POS without a $300+ software bill or a 3-year contract.

Stay on Toast if…

You run high-volume delivery with DoorDash Drive dispatch, need Toast Payroll + loyalty + marketing automation from one vendor, and want a dedicated account manager. Toast earns its price for that operator.

Migration in plain English

It's a one-evening switch.

Export your existing menu to CSV. Import it into ChowHub with the bulk loader. Connect your Stripe or Square account (you can keep the one you already use). Pick a slow weekday — Tuesday afternoon works for most — and switch over. Run both systems in parallel for the first lunch if it helps you sleep. We can answer migration questions on email at any point.
