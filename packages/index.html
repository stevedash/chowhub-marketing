FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Do you charge per transaction?

No. ChowHub charges a flat monthly fee only. You keep 100% of every sale.

What hardware do I need?

ChowHub comes pre-installed on a dedicated device — just plug it in and go. It runs on any screen with a browser, no proprietary terminals required.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Open your ChowHub POS → go to System (admin login required) → copy your Upgrade Code from the License section. Then click Subscribe below, enter your Business Name and Upgrade Code at checkout, and complete payment. Once done, click "Already upgraded? Check now" in the POS to activate instantly.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrades are instant. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens if I lose internet?

ChowHub runs locally on your hardware. You can take orders and process cash payments with no internet connection.

Ready to swap your old POS?
